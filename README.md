## What is This?

Skinlab UK's Beauty Routine Builder is a free tool that helps you build a personalized skincare and makeup routine based on your skin type, concerns, and budget. We also curate the best current discount codes for top UK beauty retailers so you can save money on the products you need.

---

## Features

- **Personalized Routines** — Answer a few questions and get a custom 5–7 step routine tailored to your skin
- **Interactive Builder** — Select your skin type, concerns, and budget for instant recommendations
- **Curated Deals** — We test and share the best active discount codes for Lookfantastic, Sephora UK, Elemis, and Charlotte Tilbury
- **Mobile First** — Works beautifully on any device
- **No Sign Up Required** — Completely free to use

---

## How It Works

1. **Tell us about your skin** — Select your skin type (dry, oily, combination, sensitive, normal)
2. **Choose your concerns** — Acne, anti-ageing, hydration, brightening, redness, pores, etc.
3. **Set your budget** — Budget, mid-range, or luxury
4. **Get your routine** — Receive a personalized product recommendation list with direct links to retailers
